DATA & PRIVACY

1) Data retention period
We retain account records while your account is active. After account closure, we retain essential billing records
for up to 7 years to comply with legal obligations. Logs used for troubleshooting are retained for 30–90 days.

2) Delete my account procedure
To delete your account:
- Go to Settings → Account → Delete account.
- Confirm by entering your password.
- If you cannot log in, contact support to verify identity and request deletion.

3) Privacy policy personal data
We collect personal data such as name, email, and billing details to provide the service.
We do not sell personal data. We may share data with processors (e.g., payment providers) under contract.
You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data where applicable.
