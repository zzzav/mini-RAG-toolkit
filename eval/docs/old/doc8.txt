CONTRACTS & SLA

1) Contract termination notice period
Either party may terminate the contract with 30 days written notice, unless the contract states otherwise.
Immediate termination may apply in case of material breach.

2) SLA uptime guarantee
We provide an uptime guarantee of 99.9% per calendar month for the core service.
If uptime falls below the guarantee, you may be eligible for service credits as described below.

3) Service credits
Credits are calculated as a percentage of the monthly fee depending on the downtime window.
Claims must be submitted within 10 business days after the incident.
