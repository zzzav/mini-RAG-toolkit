ACCOUNT SECURITY

1) Password reset instructions
If you forgot your password:
- Click “Forgot password” on the login page.
- Enter your account email.
- Follow the password reset link sent to your email.
The reset link expires after 60 minutes.

2) Two-factor authentication setup
To enable 2FA:
- Go to Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication.
- Scan the QR code with an authenticator app.
- Enter the 6-digit code to confirm.
Store your recovery codes in a safe place.

3) Security tips
Use a unique password, update it regularly, and avoid sharing credentials.
