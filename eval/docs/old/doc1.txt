INVOICES & PAYMENTS POLICY

1) Invoice payment due date
Invoices are payable within 14 calendar days from the invoice issue date unless otherwise stated on the invoice.
The due date is shown on the invoice PDF and in the billing portal.

2) Late fee for overdue invoice
If an invoice becomes overdue, a late fee may apply. The standard late fee is 1.5% per month on the outstanding
balance, calculated daily and charged monthly, where permitted by law.

3) Request invoice copy (PDF)
You can request a copy of any invoice as a PDF from the billing portal:
Billing → Invoices → Select invoice → Download PDF.
If you cannot access the portal, contact billing support and include your invoice number.

Notes
- Payment methods: bank transfer, card, and SEPA where available.
- Include the invoice number in the payment reference to avoid delays.
