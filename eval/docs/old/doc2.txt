REFUNDS POLICY

1) Refund policy timeframe
Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date.
After 30 days, refunds are generally not available, but exceptions may apply for service outages.

2) How to request a refund
To request a refund:
- Provide your order ID and the email used for purchase.
- Explain the reason (e.g., accidental purchase, duplicate charge).
- Submit the request via the support form or email.

3) Processing
Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days. Your bank may take additional time to post the credit.
