PRICING & BILLING

1) Pricing plan monthly cost
Plans:
- Starter: 19 EUR/month
- Pro: 49 EUR/month
- Business: 99 EUR/month
Annual billing offers a discount compared to monthly billing.

2) Cancel subscription and billing
You can cancel at any time in the billing portal:
Billing → Subscription → Cancel.
Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again.

3) Change billing details company name
To update billing details (company name, address, VAT ID):
Billing → Settings → Billing details → Save changes.
These changes apply to future invoices; past invoices cannot be edited, but we can issue a corrected invoice
in certain jurisdictions.
