SHIPPING & DELIVERY

1) Shipping options and delivery time
Available options:
- Standard shipping: 3–5 business days
- Express shipping: 1–2 business days
Delivery times start after order processing (typically 1 business day).

2) International shipping available
International shipping is available to selected countries. Customs duties and taxes may apply and are the
responsibility of the recipient. Tracking is provided where supported by the carrier.

3) Tracking
You will receive a tracking link by email once the order ships.
